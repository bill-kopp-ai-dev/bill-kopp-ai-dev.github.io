    🧬 The Rigor of Biotechnology Applied to Data

    The Fundamental Foundation of My AI Engineering

    Before architecting Artificial Intelligence ecosystems, I dedicated over 15 years to Bioprocess Engineering and Industrial Biotechnology. I hold a Ph.D. in the field and have extensive experience in downstream processing and purification of bioproducts.

    The transition to Machine Learning Engineering (MLOps) was a natural path: the discipline required to optimize a complex bioprocess, validate analytical methodologies, and ensure data traceability in a regulated environment (GMP) is exactly the same required to build, monitor, and govern AI pipelines in production.

    🚀 Technical Entrepreneurship and Product Development

    My deepest work at the intersection of science, data, and business occurred at Kopp Technologies, where I served as Founder, CEO, and Principal Scientific Researcher between November 2015 and December 2022.

    In this journey, I consolidated fundamental Product Management and Data Engineering skills:

    I led the development of silica-based chromatographic resins and magnetic materials, resulting in the creation of a portfolio of 53 industrial products.

    Leadership in creating simulation software for chromatographic processes using phenomenological modeling.

    Application of Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Design of Experiments (DoE), and high-throughput methodologies to accelerate optimization cycles.

    Developed my first production tools in Python (including registered software) and implemented classic Machine Learning models applied to drug development workflows and bioprocess optimization.

    🏭 Scale, Quality, and Corporate Governance

    Robust Software Engineering requires processes that don't break at scale. My industrial experience trained me to handle high-pressure and strict compliance environments:

    As a Researcher, I translate laboratory results into scalable routines aligned with Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), while applying Python-based analytics, Machine Learning, and AI to extract valuable insights from experimental and process data.

    Acting as a Process Specialist in a large-scale soy protein extraction plant, I was responsible for monitoring Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and conducting real-time evaluations in a GMP environment. This experience solidified my ability to make data-driven decisions under pressure and connect measurement signals to operational actions.

    My early experiences as a Laboratory Technician at EPAGRI (2006-2007) and Agrícola Fraiburgo (2005-2006) forged my non-negotiable discipline with data integrity, adherence to strict analytical protocols, and process-oriented documentation.

    🔬 Scientific Foundation and Multivariate Analysis

    High-level academic research requires the manipulation of complex data matrices and signal extraction from noise:

    I managed the daily operations of a state-of-the-art laboratory, supervising a team of 6 researchers and applying advanced data analysis (EDA and DoE) to guide experimental decisions.

    I generated structured experimental datasets across multiple variables and conditions, building the analytical foundation necessary for understanding feature-to-performance relationships, crucial in Machine Learning.

    I worked on correlating material properties with process performance metrics, strengthening my ability to conduct data-driven experimentation cycles and translate complex datasets into actionable decisions.
